## Production Guidelines

### Video Production Standards

#### Visual Requirements
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Format**: MP4 with H.264 encoding
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for desktop, 9:16 for mobile versions
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps
- **Bitrate**: 5-8 Mbps for high quality

#### Audio Specifications
- **Quality**: Professional microphone recording
- **Format**: AAC audio, 44.1kHz, 128 kbps minimum
- **Levels**: Consistent volume throughout
- **Background Music**: Subtle, non-distracting
- **Multi-language**: Hindi and English versions

#### Screen Recording Standards
- **Software**: Use professional screen capture (Camtasia, OBS)
- **Mouse Highlighting**: Visible cursor with click effects
- **Zoom Effects**: Highlight important areas
- **Annotations**: Clear text callouts and arrows
- **Pace**: Slow enough to follow, fast enough to engage

### Localization Requirements

#### Hindi Version Adaptations
- Professional Hindi narration
