**WHO USES THIS:** Vendors without POS system integration

### **Step-by-Step Manual Redemption**

#### **Step 1: Visit the Store**
- Go to vendor's physical store
- Bring your claim code (screenshot or note it)
- Visit before expiration time

#### **Step 2: Tell Vendor Your Claim Code**
- Say: "I have a claim code: ABC12345"
- OR show them your screen with the code
- Vendor writes it down or enters it in their system

#### **Step 3: Vendor Verifies Your Claim**
- Vendor checks their system
- Confirms the deal and discount
- Shows you the discount amount

#### **Step 4: Shopping & Checkout**
- Shop for items up to discount limit
- Go to checkout
- Vendor manually applies discount
- Discount is deducted from your bill

#### **Step 5: Complete Purchase**
- Pay remaining amount
- Get receipt with:
  - Original amount
  - Discount applied
  - Amount paid
  - Savings amount

#### **Step 6: Done!**
- Your claim is marked complete

- Receipt sent to your email
